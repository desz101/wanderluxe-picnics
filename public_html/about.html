Our Story

In a bustling city full of strong opinions, slow traffic, and fast-paced lives, we've found two things that make the Washington D.C. (DMV) area a place we will call home for years to come: each other's company and the great outdoors. Through 6 years of marriage and life in the Capital, we've found peace in the orange sunset on the National Mall, the greenery at Meadowlark Botanical Gardens, and the blue skies above Belle Haven Park. After having our first child, being outdoors with food and family has become the easiest and most satisfying way to wind down the week.

Now we want to spread that same love, joy, and a little touch of magic to the entire community, one picnic at a time. Wanderluxe was created for lovers sharing a laugh, kids running in the grass, and friends reminiscing on the past.

With meticulous attention to detail and a passion for creating memorable experiences, we curate bespoke picnic packages that capture the essence of romance, relaxation, and refined indulgence. Each picnic is a labor of love, carefully crafted to reflect the unique tastes and preferences of our clients. Whether it's a romantic rendezvous for two, a whimsical family gathering, or a stylish corporate event, we make every effort to ensure that every moment is nothing short of perfection.
