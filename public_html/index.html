How Does it Work?

Welcome to Wanderluxe Picnics, where luxury meets love and adventure! We create a picnic experience for you topped with a dash of bougie, elegance and allure. So grab a glass, spread out on our plush blankets, and change up the usual routine. We bring the vibes, you bring the energy!
